Destiny 2: Gambit Prime trailer - List of armor perks
Destiny 2’s Season of the Drifter comes with the new Gambit Prime mode. One of the key features of Gambit Prime is the class based armor sets (Reaper, Sentry, Collector, Invader), which each come with their own set of perks. Bungie has teased some of these in a trailer for the new mode.
